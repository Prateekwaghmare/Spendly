Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect the information you provide directly, including: expense amounts, categories, dates, descriptions, and account credentials. We do not collect any payment or banking information.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide and improve the Spendly service. This includes generating spending reports, category breakdowns, and trend analysis. We do not use your data for advertising or any commercial purposes unrelated to Spendly.

3. Data Storage

Your expense data is stored securely on our servers. We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, loss, or alteration. You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly may use trusted third-party services for hosting, analytics, or email delivery. These services are only permitted to use your data to provide their specific services to Spendly and are contractually obligated to keep your information secure.

5. Cookies

Spendly may use essential cookies to maintain your session and preferences. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.

6. Your Rights

You retain full control over your data at all times. You can access, update, or delete your data through your account settings. If you have any questions or requests, contact us at support@spendly.app.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, reach out at support@spendly.app.
